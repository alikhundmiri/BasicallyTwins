The What and The Why.

Every time I plan to create a new project, turns out there is someone else doing the same thing, and there is no way I can go head-to-head against them.
This forces me to do more deep study of the market to understand if its worth investing my time developing this product.

That's why Basically Twins was created, which as the name suggests, will show you the products which are very much similar to other mainstream products, but they have their own identity.

Why we need this website

We do not have to limit our self with a small piece of pie, when we can make as many pies as we like.


This crowd based website will hold the database to this massive community and our awesome products which solve problems ranging from daily work to automation.

Check out this cool tweet by Stephanie Hurlburt

There is enough room for 10 or even 100 people to exist in their own companies and make enough money to make a living while ALL MAKING THE SAME THING

How can this site help you?

With this website's crowd sourcing ability we can estimate the size of market for any idea by evaluating the size, growth and competition within existing players.

With such a tool, it will be much easier for companies, startup, bootstrappers and Venture Capitalist to validate new markets before even starting to build their product.
